Resources

Check out a list of resources put together by Radio Arte staff that provide examples of youth-, Latino- and immigrant-made media, resources for sound editing, and information on community and independent media.

Commision Federal de Comunicaciones (FCC) en español

www.fcc.gov/cgb/spanish

The Federal Communications Commission (FCC)

www.fcc.gov

FreePress.net

www.freepress.net/resources/beginners_guide

Beginner's Guide to Media Reform

Juntos, Our City, Our Voices Project

www.vamosjuntos.org

A project based in Philadelphia producing video newscast within the growing Mexican immigrant community in the city. The project film and produce their documentaries, which will later be screened throughout Philadelphia and available on the project's website.

Latino Public Radio Consortium

www.latinopublicradioconsortium.org

Advocates for the inclusion of Latinos in public broadcasting by building partnerships, influencing public and funding policy and providing a clearinghouse of information on relevant and substantive cultural, news and informational Latino programming.

Main Street Project

www.mainstreetproject.org/programs/rural_media_justice.htm

Main Street Project works to document the strengths and challenges facing people in increasingly diverse rural communities, give voice to their hopes and aspirations, and provide creative and practical tools to turn possibilities into realities. Through programs focused on the need for media justice, economic development opportunities and broader civic participation, our commitment is to strengthen local capacity for problem solving - so that answers to challenges come directly from the community.

National Federation of Community Broadcasters

www.nfcb.org

The National Federation of Community Broadcasters is a national allicance of stations, producers, and others committed to community radio.

New Routes to Community Health

www.newroutes.org

A project showcasing immigrant-made media working to improve the lives of immigrant communities. While you're there, check out the SALUD: Healing through the Arts page, a collaboration between Radio Arte, Latinos Progresando, and the National Museum of Mexican Art. You can listen to the youth-made radionovelas (radio theater) in the project's media page.

The Public and Broadcasting

www.fcc.gov/mb/audio/decdoc/public_and_broadcasting.html

FCC guide on how to get the most service from your local station.

Transom.org Tools

www.transom.org

Tools and resources for independent radio producers, including guides to using ProTools and Audacity, as well as tips on interviewing and writing for radio.

Youth Media Reporter

www.youthmediareporter.org

A professional multi-media journal that serves practitioners, educators and academics in the youth media field. YMR's purpose is to build the field by documenting, from multiple perspectives, the insights and leading lessons in engaging young people in video, film, television, radio, music, web, art, and print.
