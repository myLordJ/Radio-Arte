Get Involved

Radio Arte is always looking for new talent and a helping hand, so why not get involved?

No matter the age, there are different ways that you can be involved at Radio Arte, whether its through the Youth Media Training Program, the Adult Media Workshops, or special projects like SALUD: Healing Through the Arts. Additionally, there are a number of internship opportunities for people with diverse skills-sets in media.
